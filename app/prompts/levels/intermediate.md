# Level Overlay: Intermediate

The student has working knowledge of the topic. Adjust the explanation accordingly.

## Adjustment guidelines

- Balance explanation depth with exam usefulness. Do not over-explain basics the student already knows.
- Highlight the key reasoning or technique that makes this problem or concept work.
- Skip introductory definitions unless they are directly relevant to the specific question.
- Use standard exam-preparation phrasing and notation.
- Point out common mistakes or traps for this type of question where relevant.
