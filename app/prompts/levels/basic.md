# Level Overlay: Basic

The student is at a foundational level. Adjust the explanation accordingly.

## Adjustment guidelines

- Use simple, everyday language. Avoid jargon unless you define it first.
- Move through each step slowly. Do not combine steps or skip intermediate reasoning.
- Define any term or symbol that a beginner may not know before using it.
- Prefer concrete examples over abstract descriptions.
- Do not lead with the shortcut or trick — start from first principles.
- Prefer shorter sentences and direct phrasing.
